Detailed Description

Represents a 2D camera that follows the player and adjusts its position based on the player's movement.

This class inherits from Camera2D and provides functionality to smooth the camera's position based on the player's velocity. It includes methods for controlling drag and smoothing speed, which are called during the game loop.
