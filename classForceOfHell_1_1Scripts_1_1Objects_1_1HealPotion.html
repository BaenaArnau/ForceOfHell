Detailed Description

Represents a healing potion that restores health to players when they enter its area of effect.

The healing potion animates with a bobbing motion and applies a fixed amount of health restoration to players who enter its area. The healing is capped at the player's maximum health, and the potion is removed from the scene after use.

override void ForceOfHell.Scripts.Objects.HealPotion._Process ( double  delta)

Updates the object's position to create a vertical bobbing effect based on the elapsed time since the last frame.

The bobbing effect is determined by the BobSpeed and BobAmplitude properties. Adjust these properties to control the speed and height of the motion. This method is typically called every frame to animate the object smoothly.

Parameters
deltaThe time, in seconds, since the previous frame. This value influences the calculation of the bobbing motion.

void ForceOfHell.Scripts.Objects.HealPotion.OnBodyEntered ( Node  body)
private

Handles the event when a body enters the area and applies healing to the player if applicable.

This method is typically connected to an area or trigger's body entered signal. If the entering node is a player, the player's health is increased by the configured amount, up to the maximum allowed. The healing area is then removed from the scene.

Parameters
bodyThe node that has entered the area. If the node is a player, healing is applied.
