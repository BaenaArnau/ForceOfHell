◆ HandleCollision()

void ForceOfHell.Scripts.Weapons.Bullet.Bullet.HandleCollision ( Node  node)
private

Handles a collision with the specified node, applying damage if applicable and removing the node from the scene.

If the node belongs to the 'Terreno' group, it is immediately removed from the scene. For other node types, damage is applied before removal. The method ensures that the collision effects are handled according to the type of node encountered.

Parameters
nodeThe node involved in the collision. This may represent terrain, boxes, Balrogs, Skeletons, or Goblins.
